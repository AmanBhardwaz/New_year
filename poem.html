If I could, I would snatch a star from the sky,
and shape it into a necklace for your grace to wear;
If I could, I would bring down the moon itself,
and place it gently in your hair like a silver clip there.

If I could, I would brew eyeliner from the night’s deep hush,
let its darkness trace the stories your eyes already tell;
If I could, I would ask the rising sun to wake for you,
so every dawn glows brighter wherever you dwell.

And though I cannot command the stars or skies above,
I can gift you this — a heart stitched with words and love.
